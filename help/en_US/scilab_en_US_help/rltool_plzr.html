Closed loop poles and zeros

Closed loop poles and zeros

Description

Closed loop poles and zeros can be seen in two ways. (1) Select 'Response-->Details'. This will bring up a menu that shows the numerical values of pole(s) and zeros(s) at the current gain as seen in the slider. (2) Select 'Response-->Closed loop'. Select 'Closed loop poles' in the menu that comes up.
